We are a high school VEX Robotics team based at Vista School in Ivins, Utah. Our team is made up of students who are passionate about engineering, problem-solving, and innovation. Through VEX Robotics, we design, build, and program competitive robots that compete against teams from around the world. Each season presents a new challenge, requiring teamwork, creativity, and constant improvement.

VEX Robotics is one of the largest robotics competitions in the world and gives students hands-on experience in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics). We are responsible for every part of our robot, including design, coding, and strategy. Competitions combine technical skill with fast-paced decision-making and collaboration, helping prepare us for future careers in engineering and technology.

Vista School is a public charter school that focuses on leadership, personalized learning, and real-world experiences. Programs like robotics allow students to grow beyond the classroom. As a team, we are proud to represent our school and community while developing skills such as teamwork, communication, and perseverance.
